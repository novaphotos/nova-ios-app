What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

We collect only anonymous information

We use technologies like unique device identifiers to anonymously identify your computer or device so we can deliver a better experience. Our systems also log information like your browser, operating system and IP address.

We do not collect personally identifiable information about you. In other words, we do not collect information such as your name, address, phone number or email address.

No photos are collected or shared from the Application unless you explicitly share them via the Application sharing functionality. Each individual photo must be explicitly shared.

We do not knowingly contact or collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly obtain parental consent or remove the information.

Our service does not currently recognize the "Do Not Track" signal that may be available in some web browsers.

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

We do not store or share your precise location

This Application does NOT collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

Does the Application provide information to advertising companies?

Data not shared with Ad companies

We do NOT allow advertising companies to collect data through our service for ad targeting.

Does the Application provide information to analytics providers?

Analytics providers access data on our behalf

Analytics companies may access anonymous data (such as your IP address, device ID, non-precise location information and operating system version) to help us understand how our services are used. They use this data solely on our behalf. They do not share it except in aggregate form; no data is shared as to any individual user.

Do you disclose data collected by the Application to third parties?

Special situations may require data disclosure

To operate the service, we also may make identifiable and anonymous information available to third parties in these limited circumstances:

  1. with your express consent,
  2. when we have a good faith belief it is required by law,
  3. when we have a good faith belief it is necessary to protect our rights or property, or
  4. to any successor or purchaser in a merger, acquisition, liquidation, dissolution or sale of assets.

Your consent will not be required for disclosure in these cases, but we will attempt to notify you via a prominent notice on our website, to the extent permitted by law to do so.
